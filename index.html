OUR STORY

ROOTED IN BC FARMING

For over 20 years, our family has been crafting premium ciders and farming quality fruit in British Columbia. We understand that great cider starts with exceptional ingredients and the care that goes into every step of the process, from orchard to glass.

Farm House Craft Cider is our answer to an affordable, everyday cider without compromising on quality. Made with 100% BC-grown dessert apples, it delivers a smooth, refreshing, semi-sweet taste that's easy to enjoy. Available exclusively in kegs.

BUILT FOR

EVERY OCCASSION

Farm House Craft Cider is currently available on tap throughout British Columbia. Crafted from 100% BC-grown dessert apples, it pours crisp, clean, and refreshing with a smooth semi-dry finish that appeals to both cider enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike.

Its balanced flavour profile pairs beautifully with a wide variety of foods, including burgers, wood-fired pizza, fish and chips, roasted chicken, pulled pork, charcuterie boards, sharp cheeses, fresh salads, and seafood dishes.

Whether enjoyed on a sunny patio or alongside a great meal, Farm House Craft Cider delivers a crisp, clean, and easy-drinking cider experience in every glass.
